Classic Eats: Chicken Caesar Salad Delight!

Indulge in a classic favourite with a twist! Our Chicken Caesar Salad Bowl features tender grilled chicken, crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and shaved Parmesan cheese, all tossed in our creamy Caesar dressing. Perfect for lunch or dinner, this satisfying meal is sure to please everyone at the table. Join us in our mission to reduce food waste while enjoying a delicious, nutritious meal that's good for you and the planet!

Green Goodness: Veggie Avocado Salad Sensation!

Dive into a vibrant explosion of flavors and nutrients with our Veggie Avocado Salad! Packed with fresh mixed greens, ripe avocado slices, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and crunchy sunflower seeds, this salad is a vegan delight. Drizzled with our house-made balsamic vinaigrette, it's the perfect guilt-free meal option for health-conscious individuals.

Protein Powerhouse:Vegetarian Salad Sensation!

Fuel your body with our Vegetarian Protein-Rich Salad, packed with plant-based goodness and flavour! Featuring nutritious ingredients like mixed greens, roasted chickpeas, quinoa, sliced boiled eggs, and creamy feta cheese, this salad is a hearty and satisfying meal option. Bursting with protein and essential nutrients, it's perfect for vegetarians looking for a delicious and nourishing meal.
